# Encounters — Director's Treatment

**Feature:** BLE encounter + icebreaker (Hevolve Android — phone moment) + Nunba desktop (continuation).
**PRODUCT_MAP refs:** J200–J215.
**Mission anchor:** Humans are protagonists; the AI lowers the activation energy of the first sentence and never sends without consent. No camera, no photos, no surveillance framing. Edge-first ("your phone, your data").
**Visual signature:** Brand emerald (`#00e89d`) appears ONLY on phone / desktop screens. Background palette stays warm wood + paper amber.
**Tone reference:** Apple "Shot on iPhone" cinematography meets Wes-Anderson softness. Indie cinematic.
**Music:** Solo nylon-string guitar + piano. A minor → C major resolution. ~75 BPM. Single quiet build. No vocals.
**Cast:** Two human leads (mid-20s to mid-30s, write inclusive), one barista in deep background.
**Setting:** Independent bookstore café, late autumn afternoon.

## Editorial notes (for the production team)

- **Generate scenes independently**, then conform-cut. Most video models drift past ~15s in a single prompt.
- **Phone-screen scenes (S2, S3, S4, S5, S6, S8 of Variant A)** are best generated as separate UI-screen-recording-style passes, then composited over phone-in-hand plates. Most video models render UI text as garbled glyphs.
- **The eye-meet shot (S7 / B4 / C3)** is the money moment. Generate 5–6 takes and pick the most natural micro-smile.
- **Music brief for the composer:** A minor → C major resolution. ~75 BPM. Solo nylon-string guitar with a single piano motif joining at the mutual-match beat. Hold the resolution chord across the eye-meet → end card.
- **Eye-line continuity:** When the focus pulls from his phone to her window-table, the camera should be positioned so the bookstore aisles between them act as natural depth markers. Don't hide the distance — it's the entire point.
- **Inclusive casting note:** The treatment specifies "she" and "he" for clarity but the story works identically with any gender pairing. Cast for chemistry and for representing two readers, not a romance archetype.
- **Privacy-as-feature:** S2 (the toggle), S4 (the avatar-only card), S6 (the user-approval sheet) are the THREE moments that matter most. If a moment must be cut for time, cut S8 (the desktop continuation) before cutting any of those three.
